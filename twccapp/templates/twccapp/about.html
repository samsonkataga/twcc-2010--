About TWCC

The Tanzania Women Chamber of Commerce (TWCC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs across Tanzania. Established in December 2005, we have grown to become the leading voice for women in business.

Our Mission

To prepare Tanzanian women to unite in advocating for their rights and growing their businesses by utilizing opportunities and strategies both locally and internationally.

Our Vision

To be a hub and catalyst in economically empowering the Tanzanian woman entrepreneurs.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct in all our dealings.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and create opportunities for all women in business.

Professionalism

We maintain excellence and competence in all our programs and services.

Innovation

We foster creative solutions to advance women's economic participation.
